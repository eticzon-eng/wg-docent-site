Wizard Hat Books

Wizard Hat Books is the imprint behind WG Docent's illustrated fiction — stories told in plainspoken prose and black-line artwork, where character, thought, and the harder parts of ordinary life matter as much as what happens next.

The kind of stories we tell

Literary fiction about people living through real trouble — friendship, memory, loss, stubborn love, and the inner voice that helps you keep going. Wonder stays quiet. Humor stays dry.

How we write

Clear, direct prose with room for opinion, doubt, and the things people think but rarely say out loud. Each book is illustrated throughout in a simple black-line style that keeps the focus on the characters and the moment.

Coming soon

Silas and the Wizard — the first Wizard Hat Books title — is on its way in ebook and paperback. More illustrated novels are in the works. Get updates when new books are ready.
