Silas and the Wizard

Silas is trying to live a sensible life. The Wizard is trying to be helpful. Neither plan survives contact with Wallace, Frank, or a cat who grows grumpier by the day.

Told in clean black-line illustrations and plainspoken prose, Silas and the Wizard follows friendship through museums and kitchens, beaches and bookcases — moments where the world opens just enough to let magic slip in without making a fuss about it.
