About the author

WG Docent

WG Docent writes from a small cozy place by the water in Rhode Island. He lives with two cats who are not pets, but house companions. He believes that life is both imaginative and real. You don't have to choose one.

Writing is an idea that came to him in a dream, and the story of Silas was built around this vivid dream. His style of writing is mostly conversational. The characters are the story, and it's just much more fun to see it told from their eyes. Silas and the Wizard is his first book, and if his imaginary friend wills it, there will be more to come.
